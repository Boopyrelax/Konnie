Biography

Konnie Metaxa (born Konstantina Pantazi) is born the 10th of February 1991 at Athen. She is the daughter of Lefteris Pantazis, a famous Greek singer, and Zoza Metaxa a Greek actress.
Surrounded by arts her whole life, it is at the young age of X that she knew it would be her profession and beyond that: her passion.
After the international school of X, Konnie went to achieved a BFA Acting at the University of Miami. She had the chance to work with amazing professional artists who let her the opportunity to be a part of major shows search as "King Lear" at the Ring theater or "The house of Bernarda Alba" at the Adrienne Archt Center.
Konstantina chose to use her Mother name as her own artist name to show that her succes is hers. Konnie Metaxa carrier is just getting started, and we can't wait to see what will come next...
